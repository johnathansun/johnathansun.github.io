Merry Christmas!

My first idea was to make some art. However, I couldn't easily find art supplies. Besides, we have a lot of art anyways, and everyone is always going on about how AI-generated art is going to replace human artists. There is just a wash of beautiful AI-generated art out on the web, but there aren't as many pieces done in collaboration between humans and machines. So, I decided to tailor an implementation of a paper on recurrent neural networks (RNNs) from 2017 so we can make art in collaboration with artificial intelligence.

Hence, I present to you a few cool tools that you can use anytime. I like to think of it as assisted doodling. Merry Christmas, and have fun!
